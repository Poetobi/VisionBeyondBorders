Who We Are

Go beyond the expected

If you’re looking for an exceptional Canada visa consulting service provider you can vouch for, you are in the right place. Vision Beyond Borders is an expert Canadian training, migration, business and investment consulting firm situated in Lagos, Nigeria. We understand the pressure that comes with planning to relocate and we are committed to ensuring your travel application and preparation is hassle free. Vision Beyond Borders has been in business for 8 years with over 50 successful visa applications and we will stop at nothing to ensure our customers are highly satisfied. Our wide range of knowledge, experience and expertise ensures that you receive advice and representation that you can trust. We will listen to your concerns, seek to understand your needs, assess your options and help to develop an appropriate plan or solution to meet your goals. The Canadian government opens opportunities to all intending immigrants all over the world seeking employment, education, business ventures or permanent residence in Canada. Hence, we have channeled our services in such a way that they will suite your needs. Our Visa application services include: Canada Citizenship and Permanent residence, Study, Migration/Work and Visitation Visas respectively.
